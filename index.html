Bridging Bangladesh and Japan through language education

Our History

Founded in 2015 by a group of Japanese language enthusiasts and educators, Nihon Academy began with a simple mission: to make quality Japanese language education accessible to Bangladeshi students.

Starting with just 20 students in a small classroom in Dhanmondi, we have grown to become Bangladesh's most trusted Japanese language institution with over 2,000 successful graduates.

Our graduates are now studying in prestigious Japanese universities, working in top Japanese companies, and serving as cultural ambassadors between our two nations.

Key Milestones

  • 2015: Academy established
  • 2017: JICA partnership
  • 2019: 1000th student milestone
  • 2021: Online classes launched
  • 2023: University partnerships
